❓ FAQ

Questions we get every week

What's included in the free audit?+
The free audit covers your full checkout funnel, page speed and Core Web Vitals, mobile UX review, and your key ad landing pages. You'll receive a written report with prioritised fixes ranked by estimated revenue impact — delivered within 5 working days.
How long before I see results?+
Quick-win fixes from the audit can go live in the first week. Meaningful conversion lifts from A/B testing typically show within 30–60 days, depending on your traffic volume. We share clear timelines before we start.
Do you work with Shopify and WooCommerce?+
Yes — both. We also work with Shopify Plus and custom ecommerce stacks. Our process is platform-agnostic; we adapt our testing tools and implementation to your specific setup.
How does the retainer work?+
After the audit, we offer monthly CRO retainers where we manage the full testing roadmap — hypothesis, design, dev, analysis and reporting. No lock-in contracts; we work month-to-month.
Who is this for?+
We work best with stores doing ₹15L+ monthly revenue. Below that, traffic may be too low for A/B tests to reach statistical significance quickly. Book anyway — if the timing isn't right, we'll tell you honestly.
My OwlEye Score™ beta score keeps changing+
OwlEye Score™ is currently in beta and improving. The score is calculated in real time by evaluating 25+ parameters across 6 pillars. It may change due to temporary unavailability of some parameters dependent on third-party connections.
